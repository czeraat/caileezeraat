ABC Cocina

March 20, 2024

ABC Cocina, Chef Jean-Georgesâ€™ Latin-inspired concept, hosts a wonderfully unique menu in a stunning space. Walking in behind a group of four, I overheard a reservation mixup between Cocina, and its American cousin next door, ABC Kitchen. However, during Saturday brunch, no reservation was needed. Homely seashells adorned the walls, a contrast from the industrial concrete they were stuck to.

Met with an almost empty restaurant, I chose a secluded table in front of the window, with my very own step-up. Sticking to the brunch options, I settled on the crunchy spiced Mexican french toast ($23). While waiting for my meal, I sipped on some grapefruit juice ($7), as the $14 price tag attached to the house-made mixed juices did not seem justified by the elementary flavor combinations.

The french toast was plated with a dusting of powdered sugar and a side of syrup. I was met with two comically large slices of bread, fried beautifully golden and crisp. Poured over the entire plate was a strawberry reduction, with pieces of soft strawberries on top of the slices. My first bite was a blast: the uber-sweet strawberry balanced out with the crunchy nuts and spices on the crust. I made the mistake of dunking my second piece into the side of syrup, which made it entirely too sweet.

As I got to the center of the large slice, the bread became dry, as if the batter had not been given enough time to penetrate the entire piece. I found myself scraping up the strawberry sauce with each bite to moisten the flavorless bread, which overwhelmed my palette with the taste of strawberry instead of the intricately-spiced exterior bites I was longing for. At this point, I decided to put in an order of bacon to get a break from the sweetness of the french toast.

The bacon was cooked well, however, it was just bacon. I appreciated the four thick slices for the $10 price tag, but I was expecting some sort of interesting twist from the Michelin-starred chef here. Alas, just some delicious bacon.

I would come back to try the cocktail list and the tacos at dinner time. Staff was kind and knowledgeable about the restaurant. My first impressions may have been misled as the decor left me in awe, but the brunch itself was forgettable.

Rating: 70/100
