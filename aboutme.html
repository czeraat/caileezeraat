about me

recipes and
reviews!

Growing up around food, I loved to develop opinions on it. Whether it was my mom’s home-cooked Adobo or a 6oz filet at Ruth’s Chris, I always had something to say. After years of eating out, developing my culinary skills, and bunch of writing, I am combining my love for all these things into a single blog.

I am currently based in Northern Virginia for the summer, but I attend school in New York City. Living in two places with some of the best food scenes in the world, I have learned so much about food and culture that I am excited to share with you all. Cheers!
