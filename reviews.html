Let's Meat

November 16, 2023

Searching for an All-You-Can-Eat Korean BBQ spot in Midtown Manhattan was a surprising challenge. My Yelp results were filled with $100+ combo menus, so when I stumbled upon the $42.99 AYCE offering at Let’s Meat, I knew I had to take advantage of the rare option. Note that Let’s Meat does not allow single diners, so if you are looking to try this restaurant, plan to bring a date.

Rating: 80/100

Ikyu

January 5, 2024

Walking down 2nd Avenue on a Friday afternoon, I was not expecting to stumble on a charming, new sushi restaurant. Ikyu brands itself as “Sushi and Asian Fusion,” but while walking past those seated on my way to the back corner of the restaurant, I only noticed one table who had ordered anything other than sushi. When you first walk into Ikyu, you are immediately greeted with a stunning display of raw fish. The decor is as fresh as the food, with an airy feel, despite the lack of natural light in the tiny space.

Rating: 90/100

ABC Cocina

March 20, 2024

ABC Cocina, Chef Jean-Georges’ Latin-inspired concept, hosts a wonderfully unique menu in a stunning space. Walking in behind a group of four, I overheard a reservation mixup between Cocina, and its American cousin next door, ABC Kitchen. However, during Saturday brunch, no reservation was needed. Homely seashells adorned the walls, a contrast from the industrial concrete they were stuck to.

Rating: 70/100
